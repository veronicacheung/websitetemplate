Date: 29 October 2018
Venue: MSS Auditorium

 

On the 29th of September, some of our senior form students have joined the Blood Donation, organised by the Red Cross.
It is a very meaningful event since we are actually helping patients of Hong Kong that needs blood, thus contributing to our society and the needy.

We thank God for giving us this opportunity to help other people with our own blood. We have learned to treasure our health and pay attention to the weaker brothers and sisters in our world.
